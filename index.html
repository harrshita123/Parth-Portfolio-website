ABOUT

Meet Dr. Parth

A dedicated Neurosurgeon committed to providing exceptional patient care through advanced diagnosis and personalized treatment

Dr. Parth is DNB Neurosurgoen with more than 10 years of experience specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders. He completed his medical degree from M P Shah medical college and his Neurosurgery residency from Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani hospital and research centre.

With a focus on patient-centered care, Dr. Parth combines cutting-edge medical knowledge with compassionate treatment approaches. He believes in thoroughly explaining diagnoses and treatment options, ensuring patients feel informed and empowered throughout their healthcare journey.

His area of expertise include brain tumour surgery, traumatic brain injury, Trigeminal neuralgia surgery, epilepsy surgery, hydrocephalus shunt procedure, aneurysm clipping surgery, spine fixation, complex spinal surgery, minimal invasive spine surgery, Microdiscectomy, brain infection management and revision spine surgery.

MIS Discectomy

Aneurysm Clipping

Spinal Tumour Surgery

Spinal Fusion
